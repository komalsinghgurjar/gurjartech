How to Forward All Calls in Jio?

Steps:

  1. Open the dialer of your phone. (Works on any phone with jio SIM)
  2. Dial *401*TargetMobNumber (Example: *401*8756578855). Call can be forwarded to any operator, but your number should jio and have outgoing calls service active.
  3. Done! All your incoming calls will be forwarded unconditionally to the entered target number.
  4. To disable it, simply dial *402, and all your calls will be normal again and forwarding will be disabled.
Note:

The dailing code in step 2 will divert all incoming calls unconditionally from your jio SIM to the target SIM, but you will be charged for forwarding just like you charged for making a phone call (In jio plans call are unlimited & free so no need to worry, no additional charges for the feature to use).

If you don't have balance or outgoing facilities, then call divert will fail, and neither your device will ring so nobody will be able to dail to your number.

In simple words you need outgoing services to forward call and no additional charge. ✅✅
