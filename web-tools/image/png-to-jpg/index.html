PNG to JPG


Introduction

This simple online tool will allows you to convert your PNG images to JPEG/JPG format effortlessly and without loosing quality. Upload your PNG file above, and then press the "Convert to JPEG" button, and your high quality image will be download. PNG is lossless format but jpg is lossy format so some quality is going to destroyed anyway to convert from .png to .jpg by using any means, but this tool designed to minimize the loss at much as possible.

How to use the PNG to JPG Converter:

  1. Click on the "Choose PNG file" button & select your PNG image.
  2. Press on the "Convert to JPEG" button to initiate the conversion process locally on browser.
  3. Your converted image will be downloaded automatically once completed.
