JPG to PNG


Introduction

This online tool will allows you to convert your JPG images to PNG format effortlessly and without losing quality. Upload your JPG file above, and then press the "Convert to PNG" button, and your high-quality image will be downloaded. JPG is a lossy format, but PNG is lossless, so some quality needs to be destroyed in the conversion process. This tool is designed to minimize the loss as much as possible. After convertion PNG will be lossless when editing & using in lossless manner.

How to use the JPG to PNG Converter:

  1. Click on the "Choose JPG file" button & then select your JPG image.
  2. Press on the "Convert to PNG" button to initiate the conversion process locally on the browser.
  3. Your converted image will be downloaded automatically once completed.
